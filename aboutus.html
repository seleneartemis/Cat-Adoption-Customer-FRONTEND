"It is all about rescuing"

❮ ❯

Animal rescuers work with wildlife or domesticated pets to heal injuries or find them new homes. The field has its stresses, such as seeing the results of animal abuse and abandonment, but the opportunity to save animals from danger or cruelty is gratifying. Animal rescue organizations bring cats in from the cold almost as often as dogs. Both kittens and cats get placed in shelters because they're allowed to roam out of the house and got lost. Pet parents not wanting their cats or abusing their cats are other reasons why they are taken into shelters.
